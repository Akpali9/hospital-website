News, Events and Media

Read All About Us

The University of Kansas Health System in Kansas City is a world-class academic medical center and destination for complex diagnoses and care. We offer more options for patients with serious conditions because of our expertise and leadership in patient care, medical research and education.

Our physicians are researchers and educators expanding the boundaries of medical knowledge. Their major breakthroughs lead to the life-changing treatments and technologies of the future.

Medical News Network

The Medical News Network® is a news feed exclusively created for media. Reporters, producers and editors can access HD b-roll, patient profiles and archive video to create your own medical news reports and request LIVE shots.
