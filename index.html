At risk for prostate cancer?

Prostate cancer is the second leading cause of cancer death in US men. There are often no symptoms until it has advanced. When caught early and treated, most men have a successful outcome.

It’s a fantastic partnership between 2 organizations at the top of their game.

Care for orthopedic injuries without an appointment

From fall sports to weekend races, it’s a great time to be active. If an injury slows you down, visit one of 2 orthopedic walk-in care locations at the health system. They’re open late on weekdays and during the day on Saturday. Get treatment for fractures, dislocations, sprains and strains. There’s also an opportunity on Saturday mornings to get care for sport-related concussions.

Learn more
Ovarian cancer awareness month

Ovarian cancer is one of the leading causes of cancer deaths among women. At The University of Kansas Cancer Center, gynecologic cancer specialists provide advanced methods for screening and diagnosis, as well as leading-edge treatments.
