For Professionals

The University of Kansas Health System is here to collaborate with you. As the region’s largest multispecialty physician practice, we deliver life-changing treatments for even the most complex conditions.

How to refer a patient

When you refer to the health system, your patient will receive world-class care in a compassionate environment – and you will receive streamlined communication about their care.

Choose any of the following ways to refer to the health system:

Fax a general referral formSign in to EpicCare LinkFax a radiology referral formCall 913-588-5862, toll-free 877-588-5862.

Physician resources

Physician wellness

Your well-being is our priority. Our wellness team offers counseling, administrative support and other resources to serve you.

Share radiology results

Nuance PowerShare is an advanced image-sharing platform that allows you to upload and view diagnostic images online or on your mobile device. This system makes it easier for you to communicate with referring physicians.

Physician newsletter

The University of Kansas Health System publishes a dedicated provider newsletter with provider-focused news and updates. Subscribe to learn the latest in technology, disease treatment, recommendations and more.

Emergency services

Time-critical diagnoses: Level I Trauma

The health system is proud to be the region's only nationally verified Level I Trauma Center. We offer 24/7 care, including round-the-clock surgical critical care.

Resources for EMS partners

At the health system, we value our partnership with EMS professionals. We offer several resources to help you provide essential healthcare.

Transfer center triage

Our experienced triage nurses are committed to facilitating streamlined patient transfers that optimize quality and efficiency. Call 877-738-7286 to request a transfer evaluation or click the link below to learn more.

Education programs

Education and training

The University of Kansas Health System provides publications, training programs and other resources for medical professionals.

Continuing Medical Education (CME)

Gain new knowledge or review your existing skills with insightful, 30-minute sessions brought to you by The University of Kansas Health System and The University of Kansas Cancer Center physicians.

Residencies and fellowships

As a resident at The University of Kansas Health System, you will expand your knowledge, sharpen your leadership skills and develop responsibility and academic relevance.

To receive notifications about our CME credits and our monthly live grand rounds webinars, please contact the Physician Relations team.
