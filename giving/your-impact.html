Give hope. ♡ Donate now.

With a gift to The University of Kansas Health System, you can make a significant difference. Through your contribution, you empower scientific and medical achievement. Every donation facilitates collaboration between some of the best minds in medicine, bringing forward breakthroughs across cancer, brain health, cardiology, nursing care and many other specialties. We rely 100% on donors like you to help us transform the future of academic medicine and, ultimately, save lives and improve the health of every patient who walks through our doors.
